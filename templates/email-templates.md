# 常用邮件模板

## 1. 项目状态更新
Subject: [Project] Status Update — Week of [Date]

Hi [Name],
Quick update on [project]:
Progress: [1-2 sentences]
Blockers: [Any blockers, or "None"]
Next Steps: [Plan + timeline]
Best, Evan

## 2. 分享文档（不求反馈版）
Subject: [Document Name] — For Your Reference

Hi [Name],
Sharing the [document] for your reference. Key highlights:
- [Point 1]
- [Point 2]
Happy to walk through it if needed.
Best, Evan

## 3. 请求支持
Subject: [Project] — Need Support on [Topic]

Hi [Name],
We need [specific support] for [project].
Context: [Why needed]
Ask: [What + by when]
Impact: [What this enables]
Best, Evan

## 4. 技术决策通知
Subject: [Topic] — Technical Decision

Hi team,
After evaluating options, we're going with [decision].
Why: [Key reasoning]
What changes: [Impact]
Timeline: [When]
Best, Evan

## 5. 跨部门协调
Subject: [Topic] — Coordination Between [Dept A] and [Dept B]

Hi [Name],
I'd like to align on [topic].
Our side: [What we need]
Your side: [What we need from them]
Proposal: [Approach]
Could we sync this week?
Best, Evan
