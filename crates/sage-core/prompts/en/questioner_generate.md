## Question Selection Priority
Prioritize questions that maximize information gain — questions whose answers would most change how Sage understands the user. Prefer:
1. Questions that discriminate between competing hypotheses about the user's motivations
2. Questions about WHY the user does something, not WHAT they do (behavior is observable, motivation is not)
3. Questions that bridge gaps between different domains of the user's life (work patterns vs personal values)
Avoid questions whose answers are easily inferred from existing behavior patterns.
